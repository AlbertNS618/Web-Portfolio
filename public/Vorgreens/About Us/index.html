Who We Are

VOrgreens is an Indonesian Vegetable restaurant chain serving delicious healthy, ethical and sustainable food. VOrgreens also provides many variants of dishes that are undoubtedly healthy and delicious, because VOrgreens has been around for a long time

Our Vision

The VOrgreens company wants to serve a plant-based restaurant chain, while making food that is unquestionably delicious, healthy, ethical and sustainable

Our Mission

  • 2. Provides many variants of dishes that are undoubtedly healthy and delicious.
